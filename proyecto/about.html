About
Us


Cheese's Christ's is a local fast-food corporation in the Dominican Republic, our purpose is to feed and foster communities. One of our mottos is "cheesin' it" therefore making emphasis on our main ingredient which we use in the production of the food we sell to our clients. As fast-food owners, we make a great effort to have great customer service, which is essential to our growth. One of our main goals is to make YOU feel welcomed and satisfied by our service. Our core values which we hold deeply include teamwork and family, excellence and respect, things that are very important for the growth and the performance of our corporation.
